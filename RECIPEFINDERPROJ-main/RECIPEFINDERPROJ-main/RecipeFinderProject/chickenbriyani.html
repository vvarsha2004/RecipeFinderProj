Chicken Briyani

Ingredients:

500g chicken, cut into pieces

2 cups basmati rice

1 large onion, sliced

2 tomatoes, chopped

1/2 cup yogurt

2-3 green chilies (optional)

1 tsp garam masala

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Salt to taste

3 tbsp oil


Instructions:

1.Cook Rice: Rinse rice. Boil in salted water for 5-6 mins, then drain.

2.Cook Onions: Heat oil in a pot. Add sliced onions and saut√© until golden.

3.Add Chicken: Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.

4.Add Tomatoes and Spices: Stir in chopped tomatoes, yogurt, garam masala, turmeric, and salt. Cook until mixed.

5.Layer Rice: Add the drained rice on top of the chicken mixture. Add enough water to cover the rice.

6.Cook Together: Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 mins until rice is done
