Kadai Paneer

Ingredients:

For the Kadai Masala:

2 tsp Coriander seeds , 1tsp cumin seeds

2-3 red chilies , 4-5 black peppercorns

For curry:

Paneer: 200 grams (cubed) ,Capsicum: 1 medium (sliced)

Onions: 1 large (sliced), Tomatoes: 2 large (pureed)

Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tsp , Green chilies: 1-2 (slit)

1 tsp Red chili powder , Garam masala , 1/2 tsp Turmeric powder

Salt: to taste , Oil or ghee: 2 tbsp



Instructions:

Prepare Kadai Masala:

1.Dry roast coriander seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, and black peppercorns in a pan for a minute or until aromatic.

2.Grind the roasted spices into a coarse powder using a mortar and pestle or a spice grinder. Set aside.

For Curry:

1.Heat oil/ghee in a kadai (wok) or pan. Add sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears

2.Add capsicum and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly softened but still crisp. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook the mixture until the oil separates from the masala.

3.Stir in the kadai masala and cook for another minute. Add paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them with the masala.

4.Sprinkle kasuri methi and garam masala. Mix well and cook for 2 minutes on low heat. Garnish with fresh coriander and slit green chilies.
