Potato Cheese Balls

Ingredients:

3 large potatoes (boiled and mashed)

1 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)

1/2 cup breadcrumbs

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tbsp chopped fresh coriander or parsley

Salt to taste,Oil for frying



Instructions:

1.Prepare the Potatoes: Boil the potatoes until soft, peel them, and mash them in a large bowl.

2.Mix Ingredients: Add grated cheese, black pepper, chili flakes, garlic powder, salt, and chopped herbs to the mashed potatoes.

3.Mix well until everything is combined

4.Shape the Balls: Take a small portion of the mixture and roll it into a ball shape. Repeat until you've used all the mixture.

5.Coat the Balls: Roll the balls in breadcrumbs to coat them evenly.

6.Fry the Balls: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the balls until golden brown on all sides, turning occasionally.

7.Serve: Remove from oil and drain on a paper towel. Serve hot with ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce.
