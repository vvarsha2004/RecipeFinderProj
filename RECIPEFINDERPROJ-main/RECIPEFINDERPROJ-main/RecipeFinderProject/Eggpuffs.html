EGG PUFFS

Ingredients for puff pastry:

2 cups all purpose flour

250-g butter , cold ice water

1/2 tbsp salt

For the Masala :

1 medium onion , 2 gloves garlic ,ginger

1 1/2 tbsps garam masala

Salt to taste, sugar

1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tspb pepper powder

2 tspb oil

Instructions to make puff pastry :

1.Take 2 cups of flour , add 1 tsbs of sugar and 1 tbsp of salt and mix it well

2. Add 200-250 grams of butte into your flour and combine till it foems crumbs

3. Add Water and mix . Once firm make roumd chappati -style balls and flatten them

4. To a bowl add 3 eggs . Beat them until fluffy . Heat 1 tablesppon of oil in a pan and add the eggs

5. Cover your puff pastry sheets in film or foil for 1-2 hours for best results

6. Now the puff pastry is ready to be used

For The Masala

1. Heat oil in a pan add your curry leaves , sliced onions and saute for a minute. Once onions are sauted add ginger garlic paste and stir the mixture for a few minutes

2.Add black pepper powder, chilli powder , turmeric powder , garam masala and fry until the oil start to seperate from the masala mixture >On low heat , check for taste and add spice salt to your taste . then turn off the heat

For The Puff

1. Pre-Heat the oven to 210 degress . Get your egg wash ready

2. Divide the masala into equal portions depending on how many pastries you like

3. Take the pastries cut in rectangular shape . In a sheet add the masaka , half an egg.

4.Cover the pastry with water apllied to the corners and seal the puff pastry and then add the egg.

5. Then place the egg puff in the oven for 10-12 mins . Bake Until Golden Brown
