Banana Cookies

Ingredients:

1 ripe banana, mashed

1/2 cup sugar (brown sugar works well)

1/4 cup butter, softened

1 large egg

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

1 cup all-purpose flour

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/2 cup chocolate chips or nuts


Instructions:

1.Preheat Oven: Heat to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment.

2.Mix Wet: Cream mashed banana, sugar, and softened butter. Add egg and vanilla; mix well.

3.Mix Dry: In another bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt.

4.Combine: Add dry ingredients to wet mixture, stirring until just combined. Fold in chocolate chips or nuts if using.

5.Scoop: Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet, spacing 2 inches apart.

6.Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.

7.Cool: Let cool on the sheet for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
