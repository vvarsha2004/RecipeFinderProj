Potato Chilli

Ingredients:

3 medium potatoes (peeled and cut into wedges or cubes)

1 onion (sliced)

1 green bell pepper (sliced)

2-3 green chilies (slit lengthwise)

2 tbsp soy sauce , 1 tbsp tomato ketchup , 1 tbsp chili sauce

1/2 tsp black pepper powder

1/2 tsp garlic (finely chopped) , 1/2 tsp ginger (finely chopped)

2 tbsp cornflour (for coating)

Salt to taste , Oil for frying



Instructions:

1.Prepare Potatoes: Boil the potato wedges or cubes until they are partially cooked (firm but tender). Drain and let them cool.

2.Coat Potatoes: Mix cornflour with a pinch of salt and coat the boiled potatoes with this mixture.

3.Fry Potatoes: Heat oil in a pan and shallow or deep fry the potatoes until they are golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.

4.Sauté Aromatics: In the same pan, remove excess oil, leaving a little for cooking. Add chopped garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.

5.Add Vegetables: Add sliced onions and bell peppers, stir-frying on high heat for 2-3 minutes. Keep them slightly crunchy.

6.Make Sauce: Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, black pepper, and salt. Stir everything together.

7.Combine: Add the fried potatoes and toss them in the sauce mixture. Add 1/4 cup of water to loosen the sauce if needed, and cook for 2-3 minutes until well coated.

8.Serve: Serve hot as a snack or side dish, garnished with chopped green onions (optional).
