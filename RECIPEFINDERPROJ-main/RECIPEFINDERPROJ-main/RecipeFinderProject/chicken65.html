Chicken 65

Ingredients:

500g chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces

1/2 cup yogurt

2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste

1-2 tbsp red chili powder

1 tsp turmeric powder,1 tsp garam masala

Salt to taste

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1/2 cup cornflour

Oil


Instructions:

1.Marinate Chicken: In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Add chicken pieces and coat well. Marinate for at least 30 minutes (or longer for more flavor).

2.Coat with Flour: Add cornflour to the marinated chicken and mix until the pieces are well coated.

3.Heat Oil: In a frying pan, heat oil over medium-high heat.

4.Fry Chicken: Once the oil is hot, carefully add the chicken pieces in batches. Fry until golden and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch. Drain on paper towels.
