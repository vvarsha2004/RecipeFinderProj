Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients:

Paneer (Cottage Cheese) 200g (cubed)

2 medium onions (roughly chopped)

2 medium tomatoes (chopped)

1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste

8-10 cashews , 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1-2 green chilies (optional)

2 tablespoons butter , 1 tablespoon oil

1-2 teaspoons red chili powder , 1 teaspoon garam masala

1 teaspoon turmeric powder , 1 tablespoon coriander powder

1 tablespoon dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)

¼ cup fresh cream , Salt to taste



Instructions:

1.Prepare the gravy base:Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a pan and add the chopped onions, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and cashews. Sauté until the onions turn golden.Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.Let the mixture cool, then blend it into a smooth paste.

2.Let the mixture cool, then blend it into a smooth paste.Make the masala:Heat butter in the same pan, and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add the prepared onion-tomato paste.Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and coriander powder. Cook the masala for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates from the paste

3.Add paneer:Pour a little water (¼ to ½ cup) to adjust the gravy consistency.Add the paneer cubes and let it simmer for 5 minutes.

4.Finishing touches:Stir in the garam masala, dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi), and fresh cream. Mix well.Simmer for 2-3 minutes. Taste and adjust the salt and spice levels if needed.

5.Garnish and serve:Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
