Potato Methi

Ingredients:

3 medium potatoes (peeled and cubed)

1 cup fresh methi (fenugreek) leaves (washed and chopped)

1 onion (finely chopped)

1 tsp cumin seeds

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)

1/2 tsp garam masala , 1/2 tsp coriander powder

1 tbsp oil , Salt to taste



Instructions:

1.Heat Oil: In a pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.

2.Cook Onions: Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.

3.Add Spices: Add turmeric, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Sauté for a few seconds.

4.Cook Potatoes: Add the cubed potatoes and stir well to coat them with the spices. Cover and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

5.Add Methi Leaves: Once the potatoes are partially cooked, add the chopped methi leaves and mix well. Continue to cook until the potatoes are fully cooked and the methi is wilted, about 7-8 minutes.

6.Finish: Sprinkle garam masala, stir, and adjust salt if needed. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.

7.Serve: Serve hot with chapati or rice.
