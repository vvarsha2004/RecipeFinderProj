Malai Kofta

Ingredients:

For Kofta:

200 g paneer (cottage cheese), grated

2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed

2 tbsp cornflour , 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)

1/4 tsp garam masala , 1/4 tsp cumin powder , 1/4 tsp red chili powder

For Gravy:

2 medium onions, 2 medium tomatoes, chopped

2 tbsp cashew nuts, soaked in water for 15 minutes

1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste , 1 green chili, slit

1/2 tsp cumin seeds , 1/2 tsp garam masala

1/2 tsp turmeric powder , red chili powder , coriander powder

1/2 cup fresh cream (malai) , 1/2 cup milk

Instructions:

For Kofta:

1.In a bowl, mix crumbled paneer, mashed potatoes, cornflour, salt, red chili powder, and garam masala. Knead the mixture into a dough.

2.Take small portions of the dough and flatten it. Place a few chopped cashews and raisins in the center. Seal and shape into small balls.

3.Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the koftas until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.

For Gravy:

1.Blend the soaked cashews into a smooth paste with a little water.In a pan, heat butter. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.

2.Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder, and garam masala. Stir for a few seconds. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.

Stir in the cashew paste, and cook for another 2-3 minutes. Add salt and kasuri methi. Pour in a little water to adjust consistency. Simmer for 5-7 minutes

3.Stir in fresh cream or malai to make the gravy rich and creamy.
