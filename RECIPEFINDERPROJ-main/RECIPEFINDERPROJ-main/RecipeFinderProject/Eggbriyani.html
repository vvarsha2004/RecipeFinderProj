EGG BIRIYANI

Ingredients:

3 table spoon ghee or oil

2 cups basmati rice

1 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste

1 cup onion thinly sliced

1 to 2 green chillies , 4 to 5 boiled Eggs

1 medium tomato finely chopped

Salt to taste

1 teaspoon red chilli powder ,1 tablespoon , 4 cups water

1 1/4 cup mint 6 tablespoons curd

1 star anise ,1 bay leaf , 4 cloves , 1 inch cardomom, 1 stared mace


Instructions:

1.soak rice for atleast 20 to 30 mins . Drain water and set the rice aside a corner

2.boil eggs and remove the shells and rinse them. Make few gashes using a knife .

3.Heat a pressure cooker with oil, Add star anise , bay leaf, shahi jeera, cardomoms,cinamon and mace , allowthem to sizzle .

4.Next Add onions and green chillies FRy/saute till golden brown , Add Ginger garlic paste, add tomatoes , mint ,red chilli powder,salt and yogurt . Cook Until the tomatoes turn mushy

5.Add eggs, biriyani Masala powder ,Fry till the mixture thickens

6. pour water and salt as needed . taste to check for salt and spice levels

7. Bring it to a boil and add the drained rice

8. If cooking in a pot lower the flame completely, cover the flame and cook till the rice is done completely

9. FLuff up the egg biriyani , do not stir the egg biriyani a lot .

10. Serve it gently with a spatula
