Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients:

2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old or cold)

2 eggs

1 small onion (finely chopped)

2-3 garlic cloves (minced)

1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers, etc.)

2 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp oil (vegetable or sesame oil)

1/2 tsp black pepper (or to taste) , Salt to taste



Instructions:

1.Prepare the eggs: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan or wok over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them. Cook until set, then remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.

2.Sauté the vegetables: In the same pan, add a little more oil if needed. Add the chopped onions and minced garlic. Sauté until they turn soft and fragrant. Add the mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly cooked but still crisp.

3.Add rice: Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any lumps with a spatula. Stir the rice well with the vegetables.

4.Season: Pour soy sauce over the rice and add black pepper. Stir to combine everything evenly. Add salt if needed, but be careful as soy sauce is already salty.

5.Mix in eggs: Add the scrambled eggs back into the pan and mix everything together.

6.Finish and serve: Garnish with chopped spring onions. Serve hot and enjoy!
