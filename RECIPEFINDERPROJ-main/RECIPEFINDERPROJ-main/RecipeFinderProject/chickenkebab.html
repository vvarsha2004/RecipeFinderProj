Chicken Kebab

Ingredients:

500g chicken, cut into cubes

1/2 cup yogurt

2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste

1-2 tablespoons red chili powder,1 teaspoon garam masala

1 teaspoon cumin powder,1 teaspoon coriander powder

Salt to taste

1 tablespoon lemon juice

2 tablespoons oil

Skewers (wooden or metal)


Instructions:

1.Marinate Chicken: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, cumin, coriander, garam masala, salt, lemon juice, and oil in a bowl. Add chicken and coat well. Marinate for at least 1 hour.

2.Prepare Skewers: Soak wooden skewers in water for 30 minutes.

3.Skewer Chicken: Thread marinated chicken onto skewers, leaving space between pieces.

4.Cook Kebabs: Grill: Preheat grill and cook for 10-15 minutes, turning until charred and cooked through. Oven: Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F) and bake for 15-20 minutes, turning halfway.
