EGG MASALA FRY

Ingredients:

2 cups all purpose flour

250-g butter , cold ice water

1/2 tbsp salt

For the Masala :

1 medium onion , 2 gloves garlic

1 to 2 green chillies

Salt to taste

Cooked basmati rice or rice of choice

soy sauce , vinegar , chilli sauce


Instructions:

1. Cook eggs in Boiling water for 14 minutes , switch it off and add it to shill water

2.Peel the shell of the eggs.

3.Then Slice the eggs into two(2). .

4. To a pan add oil in low flame , to it add turmeric powder , 1 tbsp chilli powder, coriander powder and masala powder

5. Give it a good mix , then stir it for a few mins

6. Add th sliced eggs with the yolks down part .

7.And saute them in low flame for about 2 minutes , and flip the sides .

8. Add pepper on top of it .

10. Voila the Egg Masala fry is ready to be served .
