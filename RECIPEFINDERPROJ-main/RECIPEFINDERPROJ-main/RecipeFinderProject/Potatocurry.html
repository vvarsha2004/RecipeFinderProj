Potato Curry

Ingredients:

4 medium potatoes (peeled and cubed)

1 onion (finely chopped)

2 tomatoes (chopped)

2 green chilies (optional, slit)

1 tsp cumin seeds

1 tsp mustard seeds

1 tsp ginger-garlic paste

1/2 tsp turmeric powder , 1 tsp red chili powder

1 tsp garam masala , 1/2 tsp coriander powder

Salt to taste , 2 tbsp oil

Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

1 cup water (adjust as needed)



Instructions:

1.Prepare the Tempering: Heat oil in a pan on medium heat. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds. Let them crackle.

2.Cook Onions: Add the chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add the ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for a minute until fragrant.

3.Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes become soft and the oil separates.

4.Add Potatoes: Add the cubed potatoes and stir to coat them with the spices.

5.Simmer: Add about 1 cup of water, stir well, and cover the pan. Let the curry simmer on low heat for about 10-15 minutes, or until the potatoes are cooked through and tender.

6.Finish: Stir in the garam masala, cook for another minute, and adjust salt or spices if needed.

7.Garnish: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
