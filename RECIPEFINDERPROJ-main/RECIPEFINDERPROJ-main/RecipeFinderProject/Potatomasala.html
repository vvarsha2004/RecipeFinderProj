Potato Masala

Ingredients:

4 medium potatoes (boiled, peeled, and roughly mashed)

1 onion (sliced)

1-2 green chilies (slit)

1 tsp mustard seeds

1 tsp cumin seeds

1/2 tsp turmeric powder , 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)

1/2 tsp ginger (grated)

8-10 curry leaves

1 tbsp oil , Salt to taste

Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish), 1/4 cup water (optional)



Instructions:

1.Heat Oil: Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.

2.Add Curry Leaves and Chilies: Add curry leaves, slit green chilies, and grated ginger. Sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.

3.Cook Onions: Add the sliced onions and sauté until soft and golden.

4.Add Spices: Add turmeric powder and red chili powder (if using). Stir for a few seconds.

5.Add Potatoes: Add the roughly mashed boiled potatoes and salt. Mix well to combine the potatoes with the onion and spices.

6.Simmer: If the masala looks dry, add a little water (around 1/4 cup) and mix. Let it simmer for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.

7.Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh coriander leaves. Serve hot with chapati, rice, or use as stuffing for dosa.
