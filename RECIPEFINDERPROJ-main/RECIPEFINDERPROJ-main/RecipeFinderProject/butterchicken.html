Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

500g boneless chicken

1 cup yogurt

1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste

1 tsp red chili powder,1 tsp turmeric,1 tsp garam masala

Salt

2 tbsp butter

1 onion (chopped)

1 cup tomato puree

1 cup cream


Instructions:

1.Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 30 min.

2.Cook chicken in butter until browned. Set aside.

3.Saut√© onion, add tomato puree and spices.

4.Cook until oil separates.

5.Add chicken and cream. Simmer for 5 min.

6.Garnish with cilantro and serve.
