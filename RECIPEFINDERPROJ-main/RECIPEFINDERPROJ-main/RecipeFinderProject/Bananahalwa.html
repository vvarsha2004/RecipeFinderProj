Banana Halwa

Ingredients:

3 ripe bananas, mashed

1/2 cup semolina (rava)

1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)

1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)

1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

1/4 cup chopped nuts (cashews, almonds, or walnuts)

1/4 cup raisins (optional)

1/2 cup milk (optional, for added richness)


Instructions:

1.Heat Ghee: In a pan, heat the ghee over medium heat.

2.Toast Semolina: Add the semolina and roast it until it turns golden brown, stirring continuously to avoid burning.

3.Add Bananas: Stir in the mashed bananas and mix well.

4.Add Milk : If using, pour in the milk and mix until well combined.

5.Add Sugar and Cardamom: Add the sugar and cardamom powder. Cook until the mixture thickens and starts to leave the sides of the pan.

6.Add Nuts and Raisins: Stir in the chopped nuts and raisins, cooking for another minute.

7.Cool and Serve: Once done, transfer the halwa to a plate, let it cool slightly, and serve warm or at room temperature.
