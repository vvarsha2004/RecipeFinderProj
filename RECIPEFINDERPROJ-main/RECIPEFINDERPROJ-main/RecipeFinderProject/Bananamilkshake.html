Banana Milkshake

Ingredients:

1 ripe banana

1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)

1-2 tablespoons sugar or honey (optional)

1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Ice cubes (optional, for a colder shake)


Instructions:

1.Blend: In a blender, combine the banana, milk, sugar or honey, and vanilla extract.

2.Add Ice: If you want a colder milkshake, add a few ice cubes.

3.Blend Until Smooth: Blend until everything is well combined and smooth.

4.Serve: Pour into a glass and enjoy!
