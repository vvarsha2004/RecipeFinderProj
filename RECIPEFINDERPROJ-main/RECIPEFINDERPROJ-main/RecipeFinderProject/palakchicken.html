Palak Chicken

Ingredients:

500g chicken,200g Spinach

1 onion,2 tomatoes

2-3 green chilies

1-inch ginger,3-4 cloves garlic

1 tsp cumin seeds

1 tsp garam masala

1 tsp turmeric powder

Salt

2 tbsp oil


Instructions:

1.Blanch Spinach: Boil spinach for 2-3 mins, then blend.

2.Cook Onions: Heat oil, add cumin, and sauté onions until golden.

3.Add Ginger & Garlic: Sauté ginger and garlic for 1 min.

4.Cook Tomatoes: Add tomatoes and green chilies; cook until soft.

5.Add Spices: Mix in turmeric, salt, and garam masala.

6.Add Chicken: Add chicken; cook until browned (5-7 mins).

7.Add Spinach: Stir in spinach paste; add water if needed.

8.Simmer: Cover and cook for 15-20 mins until chicken is cooked.
